Mechanized Metatheory for the Masses

[People] [Resources] [Papers and Drafts] [The POPLmark Challenge]

As members and friends of the University of Pennsylvania PL Club, we seek to leverage the vast amount of work on proof assistants and theorem provers towards formalizing programming language metatheory. We envision a future in which papers at POPL, ICFP, and other programming language related conferences are accompanied by mechanically checkable proofs of the theorems they claim.

As the first step towards such a future, we will soon issue The POPLmark Challenge, a suite of benchmark problems designed to determine the current state of the art in tool support for formalizing programing language metatheory.

The remainder of this page lists the people involved, resources related to mechanizing metatheory, and papers and drafts by the MMM group.
