Comments on the CMU Twelf Solution

Important: The comments here are provisional and subject to revision on the basis of further discussion.

Drag
Aside from assum_var trick and pattern encoding, little overhead in proofs for "technical" reasons. Inability to reuse common structures like lists is problematic. Will the forthcoming module system resolve this?
Accessibility
Twelf meta-theory (modes, worlds, coverage, termination) proves a bit mysterious to the uninitiated. It is difficult to form a model of what is happening because all meta-reasoning is (necessarily) based upon different principles than are used to construct the proof terms. It would be useful it the meta-reasoning principles could be codified.
Transparency
Not scored because of strong disagreement.

Overall
Most of the solution is sorely in need of better commenting. No line number counts have yet been provided. Impressive turn-around time. Looks like the proofs could be made more modular, which is good, but simply has not been done. In the next draft perhaps break reusable components into separate files and combine with a Twelf configuration file. Please review the style guide for other suggestions.
